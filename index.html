Step-By-Step Guide to Make the Perfect Pizza

The Ingredients Needed Are:
  • Cheese(Mozzarella)
  • Pizza Dough
  • Pepperoni and/or Olives
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Olive Oil
Steps To Prepare The Pizza:
  • Roll the dough into a round ball and flatten it onto a pizza pan
  • Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the surface of the pizza dough
  • Slice up the cheese and place it evenly onto the pizza dough
  • Add any other toppings of your choice, such as olives and/or pepperoni
  • Put olive oil over the pizza dough to enhance the flavor
Oven Instructions:
  • Set the oven rack to the middle position
  • Preheat the oven to 475 degrees
  • Place the pizza pan onto the middle rack
  • Check to be ready to take the pizza put when it is golden-brown
  • Remove the pizza, allow it to cool, and enjoy!
